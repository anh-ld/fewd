Granola baked apples

The perfect last-minute dessert - this simple warming winter pudding is budget-friendly and delicious served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream

Ingredients

  • 5 heaped tbsp granola
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp light brown soft sugar
  • ½ orange, zested and juiced
  • 4 dessert apples (such as Cox's or Braeburns), cored and scored around the circumference
  • 20g butter
  • crème fraîche or ice cream, to serve

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6. Mix together the granola, cinnamon, sugar and orange zest and juice
  2. Put the apples on a baking tray and spoon the granola mixture into the hole, spooning over any juice left
  3. Top each one with a knob of butter and bake for 30 mins until soft and sticky
  4. Serve with a scoop of crème fraîche or ice cream
